3.
Data Collection
+-
a) Personal Data. We and third parties with whom we may partner may collect location information in relation to a User’s Device and use of the Services (“Location Data”) with respect to you. If you use the Services, then you are providing consent to collect Location Data such as the physical location of your Device which will be used to provide you with certain features of the Services. This Location Data is collected anonymously in a form that does not personally identify you. Location Data is aggregated with data from Devices of other users to enhance the quality of the Services we provide to you. We may also share your aggregated Location data with third parties to improve our services. b) Usage Data. When you view or use our Services, we collect data about those actions. c) Log Information. When you browse our Services, you do so anonymously, unless you have previously created an Account or Profile with us. However, we may log your IP address to give us an idea of which part of our Services you visit and how long you spend there. We will not link your IP address to any Personal Data unless you have logged into our Services. Also, our Services may use a standard technology called a “cookie” to collect information about how you interact with our Services. d) Ad Data. Our Services may operate with third party solutions providers. If this platform does so, then information about the use of this Site (including your IP address) will be processed by a server of the applicable third party and can be saved there. Through the use of this platform, you expressly consent to the process of data collected by third parties in the manner and for the purposes described in this Policy. e) Aggregate Data. Certain aspects of our Services are set up to collect and report aggregate information. Aggregate information is data we collect about the use of the Services or about a group or category of products, services or Users, from which individual identities of other Personal Data has been removed. In other words, information about how you use the Services may be collected and combined with information about how others use the Services in an anonymized manner. Aggregate data helps us understand trends and User’s needs so that we can better tailor our Services. This Policy does not restrict what we can do with aggregate information. f) Analytics. We may use third-party analytics tools to collect information about the use of our Services. Analytics tools collect information such as how often Users visit our platform, what pages Users visit when Users use our platform, and the other websites they accessed prior to accessing our platform. We use the information we gather from analytics to improve our Services. Analytics tools collect information such as the IP address assigned to you on the date you use our platform, but does not collect Personal Data. We do not combine the information collected through the use of analytics with your Personal Data. Although our analytics tools may use a cookie your web browser to identify you as a unique User the next you visit our platform, the cookie cannot be used by anyone but our analytics tools. In the event that we use Google’s ‘Google Analytics’, then Google’s ability to use and share information collected by ‘Google Analytics’ about your visits to our platform is restricted by the Google Analytics Terms of Use and the Google Privacy Policy. You can prevent analytics tools from recognizing you on return visits to this platform by disabling cookies on your browser. g) Device Information. We may collect data about the computer or Device you use to access our Services, including the hardware model, operating system and version, MAC address, unique device identifier (UDI), phone number, and mobile carrier information. h) Tracking Technologies. We collect information through technology, such as cookies, flash cookies and web beacons, including when you interact with our Services or Linked Sites. i) From Third Parties. We acquire information from other trusted sources to update or supplement the information you provided, or we collected automatically. j) Personally Identifying Information (PII). This is a sub-set of personal data that is given a higher level of protection. We will not collect PII about you without first obtaining your consent. Provided you consent, your PII may only be used and disclosed for purposes relating to the primary purpose for which the PII was collected, including allowing you to create an account on our Platform. PII may also be used or disclosed if required or authorized by law.
